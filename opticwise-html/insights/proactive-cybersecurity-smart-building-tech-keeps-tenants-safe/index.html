Proactive Cybersecurity: How Smart Building Tech Keeps Tenants Safe and Secure from Emerging Threats

Discover how proactive cybersecurity and smart building technology protect tenants from emerging threats, ensuring safety and security in modern spaces.

February 10, 2025 · 3 min read

TL;DR: Smart buildings expand the cybersecurity attack surface. Proactive, owner-governed cybersecurity keeps tenants and assets safe from emerging threats — across networks, IoT, and tenant systems. Owners without a single owner-controlled foundation can't credibly claim coverage; vendor handoffs become attack paths.

In today's fast-paced world where AI, IoT, and constant connectivity take center stage, cybersecurity is not only a vital factor— it's an urgent requirement for Proptech and Smart Buildings.

As smart technologies become the backbone of modern-day living and working environments, the potential for innovation in building intelligence is limitless— but so are the risks. Cybercriminals have adapted their tactics, exploiting the very systems designed to improve convenience and efficiency. The FBI’s recent warning, highlighted in their tweet on X about the latest Cybersecurity Advisory (FBI Tweet), underlines the growing and ever-present threat of cyberattacks on critical digital infrastructure and connected devices.

Tenants and building operators rely on proptech and smart building technologies for everything from operational efficiency to personal safety. This is why security isn’t just a feature but an absolute necessity, because it makes any and all potential vulnerabilities a disaster. The convergence of AI, IoT, or in our world, BoT® (Building of Things®), and proptech demand solutions that don’t just react to threats but proactively defend against them.

5S® User Bubble: A Fortress of Security for Tenants

At OpticWise, we recognize the evolving landscape and the stakes they hold. This is why we provide Next-Generation Firewall (NGFW) with Unified Threat Management (UTM) for each client site, and every user is in their own private, secure VLAN on a SIC® (security, connectivity, infrastructure) architected digital infrastructure.

In other words, the 5S® User Bubble — a complete environment built around 5 pillars of seamless mobility, security, stability, speed, and service, hence 5S®. It’s a revolutionary solution designed to protect every tenant while maintaining privacy, seamless connectivity, and operational efficiency. Every user operates in their own private, secure VLAN, ensuring complete cyber isolation from others in the building. This prevents lateral attacks that could exploit shared networks, addressing key vulnerabilities highlighted in the FBI Advisory.

Our architecture and deployment of a comprehensive cybersecurity arsenal at every client site offers unparalleled protection. This layered defense approach ensures tenants, CRE owners, and their employees are protected from phishing attempts, ransomware, and many other forms of cyber intrusion.

Information Security

Information security is a critical concern for any CRE property in the twenty-first century. It includes protection from unauthorized digital access or damage and can be broadly described as including the following:

  • Cybersecurity specifically protects digital systems and networks from cyber threats or malicious intruders.
  • Data security is a subset of cybersecurity focused solely on protecting data from unauthorized access, modification, or disclosure.
  • Identity management refers to verifying who a user is before granting access to sensitive information or systems by managing and monitoring their digital identities.

Long-term information security success comes only from working with vendors, contractors, and consultants who prioritize data security and privacy to protect a unified digital infrastructure, its systems, and its users. And implementing robust security measures isn’t just about protecting assets—it’s about maintaining the trust of tenants and partners.

Key takeaways

  1. Cybersecurity is a Non-Negotiable: The FBI’s advisory highlights the rising threats to connected systems, making robust security measures essential for smart building technologies and PropTech providers.
  2. 5S® User Bubble Leads the way: By prioritizing seamless mobility, speed, service, and stability —with security at its core —OpticWise ensures tenants and CRE owners a secure, uninterrupted digital experience.
  3. Proactive Solutions Build Trust: OpticWise’s innovative approach to cybersecurity empowers CRE owners to stay ahead of evolving threats and create safe, smart, and resilient spaces for tenants.
