Digital Infrastructure-First Series: Part 1 Digital Infrastructure Is the New Amenity: Monetizing Your Smart Building Backbone

January 19, 2026 · 2 min read

TL;DR: Digital infrastructure is no longer a cost line — it's the new CRE amenity tenants expect. Owners who control the smart building backbone can monetize connectivity, automate operations, and turn the infrastructure into recurring NOI. Vendors who control it instead capture the upside.

Commercial real estate owners are increasingly presented with point solutions, apps, and dashboards designed to improve performance. However, without clear ownership of the underlying digital infrastructure, these tools often add complexity instead of delivering the desired outcomes. This series reframes digital infrastructure not as a utility line item, but as a strategic platform; one that directly influences ESG performance, AI readiness, tenant experience, and long-term asset value.

What makes a CRE building competitive today isn’t just location, square footage, or finishes. It’s what tenants can’t see: the quality, control, and intelligence of the digital infrastructure running beneath it all.

Yet many owners still treat digital infrastructure as a sunk cost.

Worse, they hand it over to third-party vendors or ISPs, forfeiting long-term value for short-term convenience.

Here’s what they’re missing:

Digital infrastructure isn’t just a utility. It’s your NOI engine.

Rethink the Definition of an Amenity

Smart thermostats? Common. Mobile access? Expected. Property-wide Wi-Fi? Essential.

But these are features. The real amenity is the unified digital backbone that makes them seamless, stable, and monetizable.

With OpticWise’s BoT® (Building of Things®) approach, your digital infrastructure can:

  • Deliver premium tenant Wi-Fi that drives NOI
  • Support any & every device, vendor, and system without sprawl
  • Power your ESG and other tracking and automation goals
  • Eliminate redundant CapEx and fragmented O&M

All while maintaining owner control over data, performance, and experience.

How Digital Infrastructure Becomes a Revenue Platform

Most buildings default to this:

  • ISP owns the network
  • Ops and tech vendors install isolated systems
  • Data lives in silos
  • Owners get locked out of optimization and monetization

The result? You’re sitting on a goldmine of operational and tenant value — but letting someone else collect the rent.

OpticWise flips the model:

  • You own the digital infrastructure
  • You control the data
  • You monetize this valuable asset

We design digital infrastructure that pays for itself and lifts asset value, year after year.

Why It Matters Now

Digital demand is outpacing physical amenities:

  • 82% of tenants say connectivity impacts leasing decisions
  • 70% of Gen Z renters expect mobile access and smart controls
  • ESG mandates now require real-time, verifiable data
  • Utilities and insurance rates continue to surge

The right digital infrastructure makes all of this possible. The wrong setup makes it impossible.

Final Thought

You wouldn’t give your roof to GAF. You wouldn’t let Carrier own your HVAC.

Why give your most valuable utility to Comcast, AT&T, Cox, et al?

Digital infrastructure is the new utility. And the smartest amenity you can offer.

OWN IT. CONTROL IT. LEVERAGE IT.
